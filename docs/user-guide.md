## Getting Started

### First Launch

When you open Obsession Tracker for the first time:

1. **Location Permission**: Grant location access for GPS tracking
2. **Storage Permission**: Allow photo and file storage (Android)
3. **Camera Permission**: Enable photo capture at waypoints

### Privacy First Approach

- **No Account Required**: Start tracking immediately
- **Local Storage Only**: All data stays on your device
- **No Internet Required**: Works completely offline after setup
- **Your Data, Your Control**: Export or delete anytime

### System Requirements

- **iOS**: iPhone running iOS 12.0 or later
- **Android**: Android 7.0 (API level 24) or later
- **Storage**: 500MB minimum free space
- **GPS**: Location services enabled

## Basic Tracking

### Starting Your First Adventure

1. **Name Your Adventure**
   - Tap the "+" button on the main screen
   - Enter a descriptive name (e.g., "Creek Trail Hunt", "Urban Exploration")
   - Tap "Start Tracking"

2. **Understanding the Interface**
   - **Green Dot**: Your current location
   - **Blue Line**: Your breadcrumb trail
   - **Compass Icon**: Your heading direction
   - **Signal Bars**: GPS accuracy indicator

### Tracking Controls

#### Start/Stop Button

- **Single Tap**: Start new tracking session
- **Hold**: View tracking options menu
- **Red Button**: Stop current session

#### Tracking Settings

- **Update Interval**: 5-30 seconds (default: 10 seconds)
- **Accuracy Level**: High/Medium/Battery Saver
- **Auto-Pause**: Pause when stationary

### Understanding GPS Accuracy

#### Signal Quality Indicators

- **Green (4-5 bars)**: Excellent (±3-5 meters)
- **Yellow (2-3 bars)**: Good (±5-10 meters)
- **Red (1 bar)**: Poor (±10+ meters)

#### Factors Affecting Accuracy

- **Tree Cover**: Reduces satellite visibility
- **Buildings**: Urban canyons block signals
- **Weather**: Heavy clouds may impact reception
- **Device Position**: Keep phone orientation stable

## Waypoints and Markers

### Quick Waypoint Creation

1. **Tap the Flag Icon** while tracking
2. **Choose Icon Type**:
   - 🏴‍☠️ **Treasure**: Discovered items, cache locations
   - ⚠️ **Warning**: Hazards, difficult terrain
   - 🏕️ **Camp**: Rest stops, base camps
   - 📍 **Interest**: Points of interest, landmarks
   - ➕ **Custom**: User-defined markers

### Waypoint Information

Each waypoint automatically captures:

- **GPS Coordinates**: Precise location
- **Timestamp**: When waypoint was created
- **Accuracy**: GPS precision at creation
- **Altitude**: Elevation data (where available)
- **Compass Heading**: Direction you were facing

### Managing Waypoints

#### Editing Waypoints

1. Tap waypoint on map or in list
2. Edit name, notes, or icon type
3. Save changes

#### Deleting Waypoints

1. Long-press waypoint on map
2. Select "Delete" from menu
3. Confirm deletion

## Photo Capture

### Taking Geotagged Photos

Obsession Tracker's photo waypoint system captures comprehensive location data with every photo:

1. **Create Photo Waypoint**
   - Tap the **Photo** icon in the waypoint toolbar
   - Or tap **Camera** button during active tracking
   - Camera opens instantly with location services active

2. **Capture Your Photo**
   - Frame your shot using the in-app camera
   - Tap **Shutter** button to capture
   - Photo is automatically saved with <1 second capture time

3. **Automatic Geotagging** includes:
   - **GPS Coordinates**: Precise latitude/longitude
   - **Altitude**: Elevation above sea level
   - **Compass Heading**: Direction you're facing
   - **Accuracy**: GPS precision radius
   - **Speed**: Current movement speed
   - **Timestamp**: Exact capture time
   - **Device Orientation**: Camera angle and tilt
   - **Weather Data**: If available from sensors

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### GPS Problems

**Poor GPS Signal**

- Move to open area away from buildings
- Wait 2-3 minutes for satellite lock
- Check location services enabled
- Restart app if signal doesn't improve

**Inaccurate Location**

- Verify GPS accuracy setting on "High"
- Clear view of sky for satellite reception
- Update phone's GPS/location services
- Calibrate compass if direction seems wrong

#### App Performance

**Slow App Response**

- Close other apps to free memory
- Restart Obsession Tracker
- Check available device storage
- Update to latest app version

**Battery Drain**

- Reduce GPS update frequency
- Use "Battery Saver" accuracy mode
- Close app when not actively tracking
- Check background app refresh settings

#### Storage Issues

**Running Out of Space**

- Export and delete old sessions
- Compress or delete old photos
- Clear map tile cache
- Move exports to external storage
