Privacy Policy for Obsession Tracker

Last Updated: October 23, 2025

Our Privacy Commitment

Obsession Tracker is built with privacy as a core principle. Your location data, photos, and adventure information remain private and local to your device. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data with any third parties.

Introduction

Obsession Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") is a privacy-first GPS tracking application designed for treasure hunters, explorers, and adventurers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Obsession Tracker operates entirely on your device. We collect the following information locally on your device only:

1. Location Data

  • GPS coordinates for tracking your position and creating waypoints
  • Background location to record trails even when the app is not active
  • Location accuracy information for GPS quality assessment

Purpose: To track your adventures, create waypoints, and provide land rights information.

Storage: All location data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted database. Location data is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

2. Camera and Photos

  • Photos taken at waypoints to document your adventures
  • Photo metadata (EXIF data) including timestamp and location

Purpose: To help you document and remember important locations during your adventures.

Storage: Photos are stored locally on your device. You have full control over saving photos to your device's photo library.

3. Audio Recordings

  • Voice notes recorded at waypoints for hands-free note-taking
  • Audio waveforms for playback visualization

Purpose: To allow you to record spoken notes during outdoor activities.

Storage: Audio recordings are stored locally on your device. They are never transmitted or uploaded.

4. Biometric Data

  • Fingerprint or Face ID authentication data (if you enable app security)

Purpose: To secure your adventure data and provide quick access to the app.

Storage: Biometric data is stored in your device's secure enclave and is never accessible to the app or transmitted anywhere.

5. Motion and Sensor Data

  • Accelerometer data for activity detection
  • Compass data for navigation
  • Motion data to detect when you're actively moving

Purpose: To enhance location accuracy and provide compass functionality.

Storage: Sensor data is processed in real-time and not permanently stored.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively on your device for the following purposes:

  1. GPS Tracking: Record your location during adventures
  2. Waypoint Creation: Mark important locations with photos and notes
  3. Land Rights Information: Provide property ownership and permission data based on your location
  4. Route Planning: Create and follow planned routes
  5. Session History: Maintain a history of your tracking sessions
  6. App Security: Protect your data with biometric authentication

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share your data with third parties. Obsession Tracker is designed to keep your information private and local.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services without transmitting your personal data:

  1. Mapbox Maps SDK
    • Purpose: Display maps and offline map tiles
    • Data: Map tile requests may include approximate location data sent directly to Mapbox servers
    • Privacy: Mapbox Privacy Policy
  2. Land Rights Data Providers
    • Purpose: Retrieve public land ownership information
    • Data: Only geographic bounds (not your exact location) are sent to our Backend-for-Frontend service
    • Privacy: All queries are anonymous and not associated with your identity

Important: Your GPS tracking data, photos, waypoints, and personal information are never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Your Privacy Rights

You have complete control over your data:

Data Access

  • View all data: Access your tracking history, waypoints, and photos within the app
  • Export data: Export your adventures as GPX/KML files

Data Deletion

  • Delete sessions: Remove individual tracking sessions
  • Delete waypoints: Remove specific waypoints and their associated media
  • Clear all data: Uninstalling the app removes all data from your device

Data Portability

  • Export formats: GPX and KML for use with other applications
  • Photo exports: Save photos to your device's photo library

Data Retention

  • Tracking Sessions: Retained on your device until you delete them
  • Waypoints and Photos: Retained on your device until you delete them
  • App Settings: Retained until you clear app data or uninstall
  • Temporary Data: Cache and temporary files are cleared periodically

No Server Storage: We do not retain any of your data on our servers because we do not collect it in the first place.

Children's Privacy

Obsession Tracker does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for adult users engaged in outdoor activities like treasure hunting and exploration.
